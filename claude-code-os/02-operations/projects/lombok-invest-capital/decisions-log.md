# Decisions Log - Lombok Invest Capital (LOMB)

Track all major decisions made during this project.

---

## Format

Each decision entry should include:
- Date
- What was decided
- Why (reasoning)
- Who decided
- Impact on project
- Context (meeting/source)

---
