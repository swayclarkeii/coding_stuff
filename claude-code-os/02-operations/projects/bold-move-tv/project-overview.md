## Project Scope

### Identified Automation Opportunities

**Priority 1: Payment Reconciliation System (Ambushed)**
- Current time: 5-6 hours/week (20+ hours/month)
- Process: Manual review and payment of 15-20 freelancer invoices weekly
- Tools: Google Sheets, Wise payment platform
- Goal: Automate quality control and reduce manual payment time
- Value: Free up 20 hours/month for strategic work

**Priority 2: Lead Tracking & Reactivation (Bold Move TV)**
- Current: Manual email review, basic Google Sheets tracker
- Need: Automated lead categorization and tracking
- Goal: Systematically reactivate warm leads, improve conversion analysis
- Value: Increase revenue through better lead management

**Future Opportunity: Family Legacy Video Business**
- New venture with filmmaker partner
- Need: Video editing automation, transcript search functionality
- Recommended tool: Descript for text-based editing
