## Decision-Making Principles Observed

### Client Preferences
1. **Quality over speed:** Prefers maintaining oversight vs. full automation
2. **Human touch:** Values checkpoints and personal involvement
3. **Tactical approach:** Prefers thoughtful, targeted solutions
4. **ROI-focused:** Clear about time value and business impact

### Sway's Approach
1. **Discovery-first:** Understand real problem before prescribing solution
2. **Stakeholder inclusion:** Involve people who do the work
3. **Flexible automation:** Human-in-the-loop vs. full automation where appropriate
4. **Value-focused:** Build what's actually useful, not just what's requested
