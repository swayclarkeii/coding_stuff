## Red Flags to Watch

### From Discovery Call:
- **Revenue goal vagueness** - Need to clarify targets or risk misalignment
- **Overwhelm patterns** - Systems that add cognitive load will backfire
- **Financial pressure** - Steve applying pressure that Jennifer may not fully share
- **Partnership dynamic** - Need to ensure both are aligned on direction
- **Scalability acceptance** - May need to manage expectations on live model limits

### Mitigation:
- Get concrete goals in writing
- Design for simplicity and automation (reduce Jennifer's decision load)
- Keep both Jennifer and Steve involved in decisions
- Celebrate small wins frequently
- Be honest about what's realistic vs. aspirational
