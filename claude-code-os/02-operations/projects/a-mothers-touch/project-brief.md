## Problem Statement

Nicole spends 15-30 minutes per quote on manual administrative tasks, loses potential customers due to form completion friction, misses inquiries caught in social media spam folders, and wastes time on unqualified leads. The business needs automation to handle repetitive admin work while maintaining service quality and allowing Nicole to focus on her craft.

**Critical Pain Points:**
1. Manual quote generation (15-30 min per quote)
2. Late-stage lead qualification (wasted time on unaffordable projects)
3. Form completion friction (customer abandonment)
4. Multi-channel inquiry chaos (DMs, email, messages)
5. Vague customer inquiries requiring multiple follow-ups
6. Dubsado limitations (no auto-population of key fields)

---

## Project Goals

### Primary Goals
1. **Reduce quote generation time** from 15-30 minutes to under 5 minutes
2. **Qualify leads earlier** to avoid time waste on unaffordable projects
3. **Increase form completion rate** to capture more serious buyers
4. **Unify multi-channel communications** into manageable system
5. **Automate repetitive tasks** (T&C attachment, field population, etc.)

### Success Metrics (To Be Defined)
- Time saved per quote (target: 66-80% reduction)
- Lead qualification accuracy (target: 80%+ filtering before quote stage)
- Form completion rate improvement (need baseline)
- Reduction in missed/spam folder inquiries
- Customer satisfaction maintenance or improvement

### Stretch Goals
- Integration with external vendor systems
- Advanced analytics on inquiry sources and conversion
- Predictive pricing based on historical data
- Automated follow-up sequencing
