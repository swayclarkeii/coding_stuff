## üí° KEY MESSAGING PRINCIPLES TO REMEMBER

### The 80/20 Rule
- **80%:** Business problems, outcomes, personal story
- **20%:** Technical capabilities (as enabling tools)

### Always Lead With "Why" Before "How"
- Business problem ‚Üí Root cause diagnosis ‚Üí Strategic redesign ‚Üí AI implementation
- NEVER start with technical solution

### Partnership Language
- "Let's discover together" (not "I'll build you")
- "We'll work collaboratively" (not "Our services include")
- "I'll guide you through" (not "We deliver")

### Diagnostic Positioning
- Ask questions that uncover root causes
- "What's actually broken vs. what feels broken"
- Discovery before implementation, always

### Authenticity Over Polish
- Personal stories and vulnerable moments
- "I learned..." "In my experience..." "I've been there..."
- Human connection over corporate positioning
