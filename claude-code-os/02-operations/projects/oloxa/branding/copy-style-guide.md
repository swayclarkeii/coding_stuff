## VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

### Brand Voice Attributes

**Primary Voice Characteristics:**

1. **Diagnostic** (Not Prescriptive)
   - Ask questions rather than make claims
   - Uncover root causes through inquiry
   - Guide discovery, don't lecture

2. **Authentic** (Not Corporate)
   - Personal stories and vulnerable moments
   - Conversational language
   - Human connection over polish

3. **Partnership-Minded** (Not Vendor)
   - Collaborative "we" language
   - Long-term transformation focus
   - Mutual discovery, not one-way service delivery

4. **Strategically Confident** (Not Arrogant)
   - Clear expertise without hubris
   - Admit what you don't know
   - Results-focused, not self-promotional

5. **Business-Outcome Focused** (Not Technically Impressive)
   - Lead with impact, not implementation
   - Revenue and growth language
   - Problems solved, not tools mastered

## MESSAGING HIERARCHY

### The 80/20 Content Rule

**80% of all copy should focus on:**
- Business problems and pain points
- Diagnostic discovery process
- Strategic transformation outcomes
- Personal story and credibility
- Case studies with business impact
- Root cause analysis examples

**20% of all copy can mention:**
- Technical implementation (briefly)
- Tools used (passing reference)
- How automation enables transformation
- Technical process (simplified)

**Always lead with WHY (business problem) before HOW (technical solution)**

---

### Message Priority Stack

When writing any piece of copy, follow this hierarchy:

**1. Business Problem** (Always start here)
- What pain does the prospect feel?
- What's preventing them from achieving goals?
- What operational chaos do they experience?

**2. Diagnostic Approach** (Your unique methodology)
- How you uncover root causes
- Questions you ask
- Discovery process

**3. Strategic Transformation** (The redesign)
- Process improvements designed
- Better way of operating
- Strategic thinking, not just technical fixes

**4. Business Outcome** (Measurable impact)
- Revenue increase
- Efficiency gains with $ impact
- Competitive advantages created

**5. Technical Implementation** (Optional, brief mention)
- How AI/automation enables the above
- Tools used (if relevant)
- Implementation approach (simplified)
