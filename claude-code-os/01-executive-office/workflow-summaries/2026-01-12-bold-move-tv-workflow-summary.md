### Key Pattern Discovered
**Human-in-the-Loop as Trust-Building Architecture**

**Insight:** Client initially feared full automation ("I don't want to lose control") but enthusiastically accepted automation with approval checkpoints ("Exactly. That's true. Yeah, completely.").

**Application:** Don't present automation as all-or-nothing. Offer spectrum from "AI assists" to "AI executes with approval" to "AI handles exceptions" to "AI fully autonomous". Let client choose comfort level.

**Impact:** Dramatically increases automation acceptance rate. Client gets time savings without fear of loss of control.

**Reusable framework:** 5 trust levels documented in knowledge base for future use.
