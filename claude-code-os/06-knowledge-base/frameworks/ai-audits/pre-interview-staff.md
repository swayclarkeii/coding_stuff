# Pre-Interview Questionnaire: Staff

**Send this 24-48 hours before the interview**

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** Quick prep for our call on [DATE] - 3 min questionnaire

Hi [NAME],

Looking forward to our conversation on [DATE]. To make the most of our time, could you answer a few quick questions beforehand?

Just reply to this email - bullet points are fine.

---

### Your Role

**1. What's your job title and main responsibility?**
(One sentence is fine)

**2. What software/apps do you use every day?**
(List them: e.g., Google Sheets, Discord, Gmail, Slack)

### Your Work

**3. What are the 3 tasks you do most often?**
(Daily or weekly tasks)

**4. What's the most frustrating or time-consuming part of your job?**
(The thing that makes you think "there has to be a better way")

**5. If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about your daily work, what would it be?**

---

That's it! See you on [DATE].

[YOUR NAME]

---

## What This Gets You

| Question | Interview Benefit |
|----------|-------------------|
| Role & responsibility | Context for all answers |
| Daily tools | Technical feasibility |
| Top 3 tasks | Workflow targets |
| Frustration | Pain point to drill into |
| Magic wand | Their priority |

---

## Processing Before the Call

1. Read their responses
2. Highlight the frustration (this is your drill-down target)
3. Note tools mentioned (prepare integration questions)
4. Note any gaps in answers (ask about these)
