# Pre-Interview Questionnaire: CEO/Owner

**Send this 24-48 hours before the interview**

---

## Email Template

**Subject:** Prep for our discovery call on [DATE] - 5 min questionnaire

Hi [NAME],

Looking forward to our conversation on [DATE]. To make the most of our time, I'd love some context beforehand.

Please reply with answers to these questions - brief is fine.

---

### Business Context

**1. Describe your business in 1-2 sentences.**
(What do you do, who do you serve?)

**2. What's your approximate annual revenue range?**
- Under €100K
- €100K - €500K
- €500K - €1M
- €1M - €5M
- €5M+

**3. How many people are on your team?**
(Core team + contractors/freelancers if relevant)

### Goals & Challenges

**4. What are your top 3 priorities for the next 6-12 months?**

**5. What's the biggest problem in your business right now?**
(The thing keeping you up at night or stealing your time)

**6. If you had to estimate, what is this problem costing you per month?**
(In time, money, missed opportunities, or stress)

### Logistics

**7. Who on your team should I speak with to understand day-to-day operations?**
(Names and roles if possible)

---

Thanks! This will help me come prepared to give you real value on our call.

[YOUR NAME]

---

## What This Gets You

| Question | Interview Benefit |
|----------|-------------------|
| Business description | Context for all answers |
| Revenue range | Budget calibration |
| Team size | Scope indicator |
| Top 3 priorities | Alignment targets |
| Biggest problem | Pain point to drill into |
| Cost estimate | COI baseline |
| Staff names | Interview planning |

---

## Processing Before the Call

1. Read their responses
2. Research their business (website, LinkedIn)
3. Note the stated problem (this is your drill-down target)
4. Note their priorities (frame solutions against these)
5. Note staff names (plan interview sequence)
