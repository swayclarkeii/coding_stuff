## Critical Insights from Comparison

### 1. The Power of Reflection Time
The 8 days between calls allowed Eugene to:
- Quantify his pain (80% time allocation)
- Organize his requirements (18 documents with priorities)
- Validate his need (confirmed with real data)

**Lesson:** Don't rush from discovery to proposal. Give clients time to prepare.

### 2. The "Aha Moment" Structure
Call 1: "I want Jarvis"
Call 2: "What if you forward emails and AI does the labeling?"
**Result:** "Are you fucking kidding me?"

**Lesson:** The breakthrough came when the solution was simplified to match the actual need (identification, not analysis).

### 3. From Features to Workflow
Call 1 discussed features (data room, automation, AI)
Call 2 defined workflow (forward → process → organize → checklist)

**Lesson:** Workflows sell better than feature lists.

### 4. Phasing Prevents Scope Creep
Call 1: All ideas on the table
Call 2: Phase 1 (core) vs Phase 2 (enhancement)

**Lesson:** Clear phasing builds trust and enables faster delivery.
